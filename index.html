FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries answered.

What types of feed do you offer?
We offer a comprehensive range including concentrates, pellets, crumbles, and Total Mixed Ration (TMR). Our product line covers dairy animals, fattening cattle, poultry (layers & broilers), fishery feed, and ruminant nutrient blocks including Urea-molasses, mineral, and medicated blocks. Every product is scientifically formulated for optimal nutrition.
Do you offer specialized poultry feeds?
Yes, we have a complete poultry nutrition line. Our range includes starter feed for chicks, grower feed for young birds, layer feed for egg production, and finisher feed for broilers. Each formulation is enriched with essential amino acids, vitamins, and minerals to support healthy growth, strong eggshells, and optimal meat production.
What are the benefits of your dairy feeds?
Our dairy feeds are designed to maximize milk production and quality. They contain balanced energy, protein, calcium, and phosphorus levels to support high-yielding dairy cows. Benefits include improved milk yield, better butterfat content, healthier reproductive cycles, and stronger hooves. We offer super and premium grades for different production levels.
Are your feeds suitable for all livestock species?
Absolutely! Our formulations are specifically designed for different livestock species – cattle, sheep, goats, poultry, and fish. Each product is tailored to the unique digestive system and nutritional requirements of that species. We also offer custom formulations for mixed farming operations.
Can I contact you for custom feed formulations?
Yes, we welcome custom feed formulation requests. Our team of nutritionists will work with you to understand your livestock's specific needs, production goals, and available ingredients. We'll create a bespoke feed blend that maximizes your animals' performance and profitability. Simply contact us through our form or give us a call.
